How to Redeem the Food Voucher

 

To redeem the food voucher online, please follow the steps below. If you prefer to redeem the voucher in person at the Food Collective Community Market, scroll to the bottom of the page.

 

Step 1: Open the link to the voucher you received, and you will see a $40 gift card displayed as shown below.

 

 

 

Step 2: Scroll down the webpage until you find the section shown below. Then, copy the series of numbers highlighted by the red arrow.

 

 

 

Step 3: Scroll back up the page, click on Shop Online to go to the shopping site.

 

 

 

Step 4: Once the website opens, skip the registration process and click Shop Now to start browsing products.

 

 

 

Step 5: Choose the products you like!

 

 

 

Step 6: Click on the product you want to buy, go to the page to view the product details, if you need it, add it to the shopping cart.

 

 

 

Step 7: You can select multiple items you like and add them to the shopping cart, then click the shopping cart icon in the upper right corner of the page to enter the checkout page.

 

 

 

Step 8: On the checkout page, click Edit Selection in the upper right corner and choose to pick up at the store or have it delivered to your home.

 

 

 

Step 9: If you choose store pickup, your order will be available at the Whittlesea Food Collective. We will notify you once it's ready for pickup. Alternatively, you can also fill in the address you want it delivered to.

 

 

 

 

Step 10: After completing the options for how to get your product, add the gift card you copied in step 2, and the total amount will be reduced by $40. If the total amount of the product is less than $40, the remaining gift card amount can be used for the next purchase. Then click on the payment option, enter the payment page, and select your preferred payment method to pay.

 

 

 

Alternative Option: Redeem at the Food Collective Community Market

 

If you prefer to redeem the voucher in person, please visit the Food Collective Community Market. A staff member will assist you.
