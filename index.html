how to redeem the food voucher

 

Step 1: Open the link to the coupon that you received.

 

Step 2: Scroll down the webpage until you find the section shown below. Then, copy the series of numbers highlighted by the red arrow.

 

Step 3: Scroll back up the page, click on 'Shop Online' to go to the shopping site.

 

Step 4: Once the website opens, skip the registration process and click 'Shop Now' to start browsing products.

 

Step 5: Choose the products you like!
